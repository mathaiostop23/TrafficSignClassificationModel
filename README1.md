# Traffic Sign Classification Model
A Simple Lightweight Traffic Sign Classification Model Using Tensorflow . For Training And Test Data It Uses The GTSRB Dataset Found Here : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/meowmeowmeowmeowmeow/gtsrb-german-traffic-sign . More Than 50000 Image Data Separated On 43 Classes , 30000 Of Them Used On Training , 10000 On Validation And 10000 On Test With Accuracy Of 98%.Runned On Python 3.10 With Tensorflow 2.9. 


Test A Single Image On SingleImageTest.py Or Test On Real Time Video From A Camera With RealTimeVideoTest.py.
